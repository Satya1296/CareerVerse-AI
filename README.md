# CareerVerse AI 🎓🤖

AI-Powered Career & Scholarship Guidance System

## Overview
CareerVerse AI is an intelligent career guidance platform designed to help students, parents, employees, and scholarship seekers make informed career decisions.

The system uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze user profiles, interests, skills, education background, budget, and career goals to provide personalized recommendations for career paths, scholarships, skill development, and financial planning.

## Problem Statement
Many students feel confused after completing 10th or 12th grade about choosing the right career path. Parents often struggle with financial planning and scholarship opportunities. Employees may want to upskill or switch careers but lack clear guidance.

CareerVerse AI solves this problem by providing a single smart platform for career guidance and scholarship assistance.
